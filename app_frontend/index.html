How It Works

01

SAR Backscatter

Sentinel-1 C-band radar detects metal equipment and vessels on sandbars. Log-ratio anomaly between a seasonal quiet-state baseline and target pass. Flagged area = statistically unusual radar return.

Sentinel-1 GRD · VV · 10m
02

NDWI Morphology

Sentinel-2 NDWI water/sandbar mask tracks exposed sandbar area vs. a seasonal baseline. Flags deviations beyond the historical range for that specific segment and month — not a global threshold.

Sentinel-2 · NDWI · 10m
03

Hedged Scoring

Two-signal fusion into NONE / LOW / ELEVATED / UNDER_REVIEW. The system never outputs "confirmed illegal." Confirmation is always a human, legal, or journalistic judgment — not an algorithm.

Anomaly Scorer · Not a legal tool
What this platform does NOT claim
  • NOT riverbed depth or volume extracted — SAR does not see through water
  • NOT near-real-time — see acquisition dates on every layer
  • NOT "illegal mining confirmed" — anomalies require human verification
  • NOT sub-meter commercial optical — we use free Copernicus data at 10m
  • NOT a replacement for state enforcement
  • NOT black-box AI — transparent, documented statistical method
